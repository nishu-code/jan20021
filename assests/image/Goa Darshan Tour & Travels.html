Aguada Light House  One of the distinctive features of the Aguada headlands are the Lighthouses built as early as 1864 thereby marking them as one of the oldest and first lighthouses to be built in Asia. Anjuna Beach  The beach is famous for its stunning natural beauty with swaying palms trees and soft powered white sands. Chauranginath Temple  Chauranginath Temple is Shiva temple located at Arpora,Goa. Morjim Beach  Morjim is a Census Town in Pernem, Goa, India; it is situated on the northern bank of the Chapora River estuary.
